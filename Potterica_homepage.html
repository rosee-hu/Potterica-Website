About Potterica

FAQs

Our pottery shop offers a wide range of ceramic pieces, including mugs, bowls, plates, and vases.

We use high-quality clay and glazes to ensure durability and aesthetic appeal in our pottery. Our skilled artisans employ various techniques such as wheel-throwing, hand-building, carving, and sculpting to craft each piece with care and attention to detail. If you have any specific questions about our processes, feel free to reach out to our knowledgeable staff.

At Potterica Ceramics, our main focus is on showcasing our latest seasonal collections only, However, if you're interested in a specific piece from a past collection, feel free to Contact our team.

While our unique pieces are carefully curated and often one-of-a-kind, we understand that customers may have specific preferences or needs. Depending on the piece, and artist, we may be able to accommodate minor customizations or explore similar styles to suit your requirements. Contact our team for more information.

Once you place your order, it takes 3 to 5 business days to process and ship the order.
Note that custom orders made through email might take longer time to be processed, depending on the artist and piece.
